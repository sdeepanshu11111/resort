About Us

01

Crafted with Passion, Driven by Legacy

AJ Motoarts is all about creating one-of-a-kind vehicles, sculptures, and art pieces for collectors who value the rare and the exquisite. Led by Kanwardeep and Anhad Jaura, we turn ideas into lasting works of art that honor history and showcase individual style

Our Studio

03

A Workshop of Ideas and Innovation

Our studio is more than just a workspace—it’s a place where ideas take shape. Every vehicle, sculpture, and collectible is crafted with patience and care, merging old techniques with new inspiration.

Our team selects only the finest materials and uses hands-on techniques to ensure each project is built to last. Every element is thoughtfully crafted to create pieces that are as durable as they are beautiful.
